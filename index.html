Platform overview

One platform. Two ways to run it.

Media owners struggle to realise value from a pile of point solutions — siloed data, slow experience changes, lost opportunity. Ad-Core is one integrated chassis of seven modules, available as a SaaS license your team operates, or as a partnership where we build and operate with you.

Internal integration

The loop is the product.

Every module reads from, and writes back to, the same identity spine — so day-to-day use never burdens IT.

Deployment models

SaaS license, or operated partnership.

MODEL A · SAAS

Platform license

License modules individually or as a stack. Annual license plus usage, with implementation, managed connectors, training and certification, regional hosting and PDPL-ready compliance included. Your team operates; our Academy keeps them certified.

MODEL B · PARTNERSHIP

Build · Operate · Own

We deploy the chassis, launch the revenue lines and operate them under long-term agreements — retainer plus revenue share on monetisation, JV equity on platform ventures, and a build–operate–transfer path when you're ready to insource.

Data portability

Data in, data out. No lock-in.

DATA IN

Ingestion points

Behavioural events (JS tags + server-side APIs, unsampled, real time) · CRM and subscriber-file imports, print included · Event registration and delegate data · External segments · Contextual data (Arabic + English).

DATA OUT

Export & sync

Real-time segment exports and webhooks · Clean-room matches with banks, telcos and retailers · Third-party identifier sync (ID5, UID2.0) · Warehouse and BI exports · Full log access.

Architecture

Real-time engine, regional residency.

SPEED

Event to action in milliseconds

A real-time in-memory engine for segmentation and journey decisions, synchronised with a warehouse layer for analytics and data products — one platform, both speeds.

RESIDENCY

GCC data residency & PDPL

Regional hosting with data-residency options per market, consent built into the spine, and Arabic-first data models and taxonomies global platforms don't carry.
